08
Clearspace & Minimum Size

Clearspace

Minimum clearspace on all sides = cap-height of the wordmark (X). Never place competing elements within this zone.

Minimum Wordmark Size

80px wide on digital. 20mm wide in print. Below these thresholds, use the X icon mark only.

Never Do

  • — Stretch, condense, or skew the wordmark
  • — Apply colour fills, gradients, or shadows
  • — Use weights other than SemiBold (600)
  • — Alter the letter-spacing
  • — Place on mid-tone backgrounds without testing contrast
